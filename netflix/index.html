The Good Dinosaur

The film explores an alternate history where non-avian dinosaurs never became extinct,following a young, timid Apatosaurus named Arlo (Ochoa), who meets an unlikely human friend named Spot (Bright) while traveling through a dangerous and mysterious landscape in order to return home, after being washed downriver by a rainstorm.
